WHAT DO WE DO WITH EOS

OPERATE

We have people, budget and experience available for operating the block producer infrastructure.

DEVELOP

We want to invest in building infrastructure and distributed applications, that represent real value to our community. Our investments will most likely go to apps and dapps on topics such as on-boarding, identity, reputation, security and usability of the platform.

PROMOTE

We organize meetups and workshops for developers, entrepreneurs, and investors to evangelize the power of the concepts behind EOS. We also plan to help on-boarding companies by providing lectures and on-premise training. Our team of crypto specialists is focused on helping to build and operate the infrastructure to support the EOS community.
