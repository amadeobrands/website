MISSION

As EOS Amsterdam, we live for developing, operating and promoting the EOS infrastructure. Our goal? Help people organize into voluntary free-market communities to secure life, liberty and property for all. EOS shall become and remain a viable legal ecosystem based on fundamental human rights which decisions are sound and respected by outside courts.

Accountable

Because we are a legal entity registered in the Netherlands, we can be held responsible for our actions, changes, failures and investments. Everything is communicated to our community. The Netherlands are a mature democracy and a stable member of the European Union. Located in Amsterdam Dutch and Amsterdam people well known for their liberal mind and their open view to the world. It is the mind of a people that host We feel an Amsterdam located block producer should be included, because of oursuch an important internet hub function.

Consistent

We consistently review our operations and infrastructure to find improvements. We believe in repetitively and marginally improving to end up with a qualitative service.

Transparent

Every action, change, failure and investment is registered on-chain and communicated to our community. We believe in transparency. We will seek to ensure that the arbitration proceedings comply with the principles of a robust conflict resolving system, such as principles of independence, due process, and effectiveness.

Reciprocity

We have a full-time involvement in the EOS community. Therefore, we have our ears to the ground. We know what the community wants and give back to them.

Secure

We collaborate closely with Kahuna, a managed security services provider in the Netherlands and Spain. This company will secure, manage and monitor our block producer infrastructure 24x7.

Independent

People, budget and experience; everything is already in place to set up and monitor the block producer infrastructure. The result? We are able to operate independently.
